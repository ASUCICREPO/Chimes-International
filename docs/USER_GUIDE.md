## Getting Started

### Accessing the Application

1. **Open your web browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
2. **Navigate to**: `https://main.d1an0vqq41s0dw.amplifyapp.com/`
3. **The chat interface will load** - no login required!

### First Steps

1. **Select your language**: Click the language toggle (EN/ES) in the top-right corner
2. **Start chatting**: Type your question in the input box at the bottom
3. **Press Enter or click Send**: Your message will be sent to the AI assistant

## Basic Features

### 1. Asking Questions

The Knowledge Companion can answer questions about:
- **HR Policies**: Vacation time, benefits, payroll, time off
- **IT Support**: Software access, technical issues, equipment requests
- **Company Policies**: Code of conduct, remote work, dress code
- **Employee Resources**: Training programs, career development, workplace safety

**Example Questions**:
```
"What is the company's vacation policy?"
"How do I request time off?"
"What software tools are available to employees?"
"What are the remote work guidelines?"
```

### 2. Language Selection

**Switching Languages**:
- Click the **EN/ES toggle** button in the top-right corner
- The interface will immediately switch languages
- All AI responses will be in your selected language

**Supported Languages**:
- 🇺🇸 **English** (EN)
- 🇪🇸 **Spanish** (ES)

**Note**: You can ask questions in either language, and the assistant will respond in your selected language.

### 3. Viewing Source Citations

**Why Citations Matter**:
- AI responses are based on official company documents
- Citations show you exactly where the information comes from
- You can verify the information by checking the source

**How to View Citations**:
1. After receiving an AI response, look for the **"Sources (X)"** button below the message
2. Click the button to expand the list of sources
3. Each source shows:
   - Document name (e.g., "vacation-policy.pdf")
   - Relevant text excerpt
   - Link to the full document (if available)

**Example**:
```
AI Response: "Employees receive 15 days of paid vacation per year..."

Sources (2):
📄 Employee Handbook - Page 12
   "All full-time employees are entitled to 15 days of paid vacation..."

📄 HR Benefits Guide - Section 3.2
   "Vacation days accrue at a rate of 1.25 days per month..."
```

### 4. Providing Feedback

**Help Improve the Assistant**:
- After each AI response, you'll see **👍** (thumbs up) and **👎** (thumbs down) buttons
- Click thumbs up if the response was helpful
- Click thumbs down if the response was not helpful
- Your feedback helps improve the system over time

**What Happens to Feedback?**:
- Anonymous feedback is stored for analysis
- The admin team reviews feedback to improve the knowledge base
- No personal information is collected

## Advanced Features

### 1. Conversation History

**Accessing Previous Conversations**:
- Look at the **left sidebar** (desktop) or **menu icon** (mobile)
- You'll see a list of your recent conversations
- Click any conversation to resume where you left off

**Understanding the History**:
- Conversations show the first message as a preview
- Timestamps help you find recent chats
- Language indicator shows if it was in English or Spanish

**Creating a New Conversation**:
- Click the **"New Chat"** button at the top of the sidebar
- Or simply refresh the page

### 2. Follow-Up Questions

**Context-Aware Conversations**:
The AI remembers your conversation context, so you can ask follow-up questions naturally.

**Example Conversation**:
```
You: "What is the vacation policy?"
AI: "Employees receive 15 days of paid vacation per year..."

You: "How do I request vacation time?"
AI: "To request vacation, submit a request through the HR portal..."

You: "Is there a deadline for submitting requests?"
AI: "Yes, vacation requests should be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance..."
```

The AI understands that "How do I request vacation time?" refers to the vacation policy discussed earlier, without you having to repeat the context.

### 3. Suggested Questions

**Quick Start Prompts**:
- When you start a new conversation, you'll see **suggested question chips**
- These are common questions other employees ask
- Click any chip to instantly ask that question

**Examples of Suggested Questions**:
- "What are the company benefits?"
- "How do I access the employee portal?"
- "What is the remote work policy?"

### 4. Regenerating Responses

**If you're not satisfied with a response**:
1. Click the **🔄 Regenerate** button below the AI message
2. The AI will generate a new response to your question
3. This uses the same knowledge base but may phrase the answer differently

**When to Regenerate**:
- The response wasn't clear
- You need more detail
- The AI misunderstood your question

## Tips & Best Practices

### Writing Effective Questions

✅ **DO**:
- Be specific: "What is the PTO policy for new employees?" vs. "Tell me about PTO"
- Use complete sentences: "How do I reset my password?" vs. "password"
- Ask one question at a time: "What are the health insurance options?" (then follow up with specifics)
- Provide context: "I'm a new employee. What benefits am I eligible for?"

❌ **DON'T**:
- Use vague language: "Tell me about stuff" (too broad)
- Ask multiple unrelated questions at once: "What's the vacation policy and how do I get parking and what's for lunch?"
- Use abbreviations the AI might not know: "What's the ESPP?" (unless it's a common company term)

### Getting Better Answers

**1. Start Broad, Then Narrow Down**:
```
First: "What are the company benefits?"
Then: "Tell me more about health insurance"
Finally: "What's the difference between the HMO and PPO plans?"
```

**2. Use the Language You're Comfortable With**:
- The AI understands both English and Spanish
- You can ask in one language and get a response in another (using the language toggle)

**3. Check the Sources**:
- Always review citations for important information
- If something seems unclear, ask for clarification
- You can request specific document references: "Can you cite the employee handbook?"

**4. Provide Feedback**:
- Use thumbs up/down to help improve responses
- If something is wrong, ask a follow-up question to clarify

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is my conversation private?**
A: Conversations are stored for analytics but are not linked to your personal identity. Feedback is anonymous.

**Q: Can I use this on my phone?**
A: Yes! The interface is fully responsive and works on all devices (desktop, tablet, mobile).

**Q: How accurate is the AI?**
A: The AI is trained on official company documents and provides accurate information with source citations. However, always verify critical information with HR or your manager.

**Q: What if the AI doesn't know the answer?**
A: The AI will tell you if it doesn't have enough information. In that case, contact HR directly or submit a help desk ticket.

### Language & Content

**Q: Can I switch languages mid-conversation?**
A: Yes! Click the language toggle at any time. The conversation history will remain, but new responses will be in the selected language.

**Q: What topics can I ask about?**
A: HR policies, IT support, company procedures, benefits, training programs, and general employee resources.

**Q: What topics are NOT covered?**
A: Personal employee data, payroll specifics, confidential information, and anything outside official company documents.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Why is the AI taking long to respond?**
A: The AI searches through company documents to find accurate information. Complex questions may take 5-10 seconds. If it takes longer, check your internet connection or try refreshing.

**Q: I see an error message. What should I do?**
A: See the [Troubleshooting](#troubleshooting) section below. Most issues can be resolved by refreshing the page.

**Q: Can I download my conversation history?**
A: Not currently, but this feature may be added in the future.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Issue: "VITE_API_ENDPOINT is not configured" Error

**Symptoms**: Error message appears when sending a message
**Cause**: API endpoint environment variable is not set

**Solution**:
1. Contact your IT administrator
2. This is a configuration issue on the deployment side
3. Users cannot fix this themselves

---

#### Issue: Response is Slow or Stuck

**Symptoms**: Typing indicator appears but no response comes
**Solution**:
1. Wait up to 30 seconds (complex questions take time)
2. Check your internet connection
3. If still stuck, refresh the page and try again
4. Try simplifying your question

---

#### Issue: Page Won't Load

**Symptoms**: Blank screen or loading error
**Solution**:
1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
2. Clear your browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
3. Try a different browser
4. Check if your company network is blocking the site
5. Contact IT support if the issue persists

---

#### Issue: Sources Button Not Working

**Symptoms**: Can't click "Sources" button or citations won't expand
**Solution**:
1. Refresh the page
2. Wait for the full response to load
3. Try clicking again
4. If issue persists, report to IT

---

#### Issue: Language Toggle Not Working

**Symptoms**: UI doesn't change language when clicking toggle
**Solution**:
1. Click the toggle again
2. Refresh the page
3. Clear browser cache
4. Report to IT if issue continues

---

#### Issue: Feedback Buttons Not Responding

**Symptoms**: Thumbs up/down buttons don't highlight when clicked
**Solution**:
1. This is usually a visual delay - your feedback was likely recorded
2. Try clicking once and waiting a few seconds
3. Refresh the page if needed
4. You can skip feedback - it's optional

---

### Reporting Problems

**If you encounter issues not listed here**:

1. **Note the error message** (take a screenshot if possible)
2. **Try the basic troubleshooting steps**:
   - Refresh the page
   - Clear cache
   - Try a different browser
3. **Contact IT Support**:
   - Email: support@yourorganization.com
   - Phone: ext. 1234
   - Include:
     - What you were trying to do
     - What happened instead
     - Browser and device type
     - Screenshot of any error messages

## Best Practices for Employees

### When to Use the Knowledge Companion

✅ **Use for**:
- General policy questions
- Looking up benefit information
- Finding IT support resources
- Understanding company procedures
- Quick answers to common questions

❌ **Don't use for**:
- Requesting time off (use HR portal)
- Reporting IT issues (use help desk)
- Personal HR matters (contact HR directly)
- Emergency situations (call appropriate contacts)
- Anything requiring immediate action

### Information Accuracy

**The AI is a tool to help you find information quickly**:
- ✅ Always verify critical information with official sources
- ✅ Check the citations to see where information comes from
- ✅ Contact HR for personal or sensitive matters
- ✅ Report outdated or incorrect information to IT

**Remember**: The AI assistant is only as good as the documents it's trained on. If company policies change, there may be a brief delay before the AI is updated.

## Glossary

**AI (Artificial Intelligence)**: The technology that powers the Knowledge Companion's ability to understand questions and provide answers.

**Citation**: A reference to the source document where information was found.

**Knowledge Base**: The collection of company documents the AI uses to answer questions.

**RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)**: The technology that allows the AI to search documents and generate accurate, cited responses.

**Conversation History**: Your past chats with the Knowledge Companion, saved for easy reference.
